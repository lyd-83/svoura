How it all started

Animal cruelty facts and stats

I was wandering on the streets. People was kicking me around and other dogs weren't always friendly with me.
There were days that I couldn't find anything to eat, anything at all.
Then, one day, I don't really remember how it all happened, maybe I was walking nearby or maybe someone just found me, things are kinda fuzzy.
Still, the point is that now suddenly I was in a shelter...

In the shelter, in the city

Things to know before adopting a dog

I can't deny that people in the shelter were nice, but when I got there, I had a peculiar feeling something was changing.
Was it the trees around me? Was it the fact that we were all with a leash (I hate leashes!) or separated one from the other in several ways?
Well, my friends, it was all that together and nothing at all, since it was me the one who was changing.
You know, I had a flink once with a beatiful dog, although now that I was pregnant I had to admit the fact that it wasn't just a flink. Anyway!
As you can see, my little ones had came out to the world and already had been asked from many homes.
What about me? Their mummy who was about to lose them soon? Strange thing, nobody was asking for me to take me home...
