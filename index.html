My name is Svoura and this is my story

How it all started

I was wandering on the streets. People was kicking me around and other dogs weren't always friendly with me. There were days that I couldn't find anything to eat, anything at all. Then, one day, I don't really remember how it all happened, maybe I was walking near there or maybe someone found, things are kinda fuzzy, but the point is that now suddenly I was in a shelter.
